There are many skills, methods and resources that you need to know when completing an IT project. One of the key skills needed in modern business is to keep up with modern-day software. This due to the fact software constantly updates and running on out of date software can cause your files to crash or mean you are not equipped for working collaboratively. A way to do this effectively is to constantly research what resources are available to you that could help you complete your project. Arguably the most important method for an IT project is to be able to plan your project extensively before starting it. This will help you stay on track and help you have a clear creative vision, this approach will also help you to work collaboratively.
