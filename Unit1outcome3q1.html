I think there are equal positives and negatives when using IT in order to keep on track. Tools like trello can help you stay on task, however after uplaoding everything you have to do on them they can make you stressed and actually hurt your progress instead of helping. I think I have used IT well to plan this project as I used trello to organize my work. On reflection it might be more useful find a program that generates a clear schedule for the day. As this may help me stay on strack and keep me motivated if I keep hitting all my targets. The downside of this approach is that if I fell behind this may do more harm than good. This would be an aproach I will consider in future projects
